# **App Name**: CrediPay

## Core Features:

- UPI Payment & Transaction Management: Facilitate secure sending and receiving of payments via UPI, display a large QR code for merchants, enable scanning, and provide a clear overview of recent transactions with confirmation details and success animations.
- AI-Powered Business Performance Analytics: Provide a clean line chart and visualizations for daily, weekly, and monthly income trends, peak hours, and top transaction days. Use an AI tool to generate small, actionable insights into business performance, such as sales increases or peak hours.
- Credit Score Tracking & Enhancement Tool: Display a prominent credit score with a visual breakdown of influencing factors like consistency, volume, and growth. Incorporate an AI tool that analyzes the merchant's financial data to provide tailored recommendations for improving their credit score.
- Streamlined Loan Application & Pre-approval: Show a personalized loan eligibility card, display pre-approved amounts and EMI previews, and guide users through a straightforward loan application process with options for selecting amount and repayment periods.
- Secure Merchant Onboarding & Profile Management: Implement a user-friendly onboarding flow with phone number OTP verification and business details input. Allow merchants to manage their business profile, owner details, contact information, bank account specifics, and app settings.
- Intuitive Bottom Navigation: A 5-tab bottom navigation structure providing quick access to Home, Payments, Analytics, Credit, and Profile screens, ensuring primary functions are always easily discoverable.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Indigo (`#191A40`) for professionalism and trust in a financial application.
- Background color: A very light off-white with a hint of blue (`#F5F6F8`) for a clean, minimal base.
- Accent color: Emerald Green (`#1EA366`) used for highlights, call-to-action buttons, and indicating positive outcomes like successful payments or growth, signifying prosperity and clarity.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clean appearance, excellent readability, and robust numerical character sets, which is crucial for displaying large numbers effectively.
- A clean, minimal, card-based layout with soft shadows and generously rounded corners (12-16px radius) for visual appeal and distinct information separation, avoiding clutter.
- Prominent display of numerical data using a large, bold typeface, ensuring critical business metrics like earnings and credit scores are instantly legible for merchants.
- Subtle and purposeful micro-interactions including smooth screen transitions, responsive button press animations, dynamic graph animations for data visualization, and a clear green check success state for payment confirmations.
